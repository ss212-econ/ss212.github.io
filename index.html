About Me

Welcome to my webpage!

I am a Doctoral Student of Economics at the Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence, Delhi, India. With a master's degree in economics from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (2020-2022) and a bachelor's degree in mathematics from Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur (2017-2020), I have a strong academic background in economics.

My primary research interests lie in Microeconomic Theory. I am currently working on my Ph.D. thesis, focusing on "Strategic Experimentation".
